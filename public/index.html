Matt Lavergne: Automation Specialist & Digital Services Coordinator, Lafayette, Louisiana

Matthew Lavergne is a Digital Services Coordinator at the University of Louisiana at Lafayette Foundation, where he designs enterprise Power Automate solutions, REST API integrations, and custom connector architecture that replace manual processes with scalable systems across Microsoft 365. His flagship is a 47-flow Power Automate solution automating gift processing, donor operations, events, and onboarding. This portfolio is presented as an interactive desktop operating system, "mattOS". Projects include a live Lafayette 911 traffic incident map at mattlavergne.com/trafficmap, a map background generator, a VBA PDF bookmark router, a social-to-CRM bulk import, and WiFi trilateration motion detection.
